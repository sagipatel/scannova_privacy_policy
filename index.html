Privacy Policy for Scannovia

Effective Date: March 11, 2026

Scannovia ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the Scannovia mobile application collects, uses, and protects user information.

1. Information We Collect

The application may collect the following types of information:

  • Images from Camera or Gallery
    Users may capture or upload product images using their device camera or gallery. These images are used to analyze food ingredients and product information.
  • Device Information
    Basic device data such as device type, operating system version, and app version may be collected for performance and stability improvements.
  • Usage Data
    Anonymous usage information may be collected to improve the user experience.

Scannovia does not collect personal identifiers such as name, phone number, or address.

2. Health Data Processing

Scannovia analyzes food and product ingredient information to provide insights about potential health impact such as nutritional information, ingredient safety, or health-related warnings.

This information may be considered health-related data under Google Play policies.

Health-related analysis is generated using AI based on the scanned product image and ingredient information.

This data is used only to provide informational health insights to the user and is not used for medical diagnosis, treatment, or prevention of disease.

3. Camera Permission

The app requires camera permission so users can scan product packaging or ingredient labels. Images are processed only for ingredient analysis.

4. How We Use the Information

  • Analyze food ingredients using AI
  • Provide nutritional and health insights
  • Improve application performance
  • Fix bugs and improve user experience

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share user data with advertisers or third parties.

Images may be temporarily processed by secure third-party AI services only to generate analysis results.

6. Data Retention

Images scanned by the user are processed temporarily and are not permanently stored on our servers.

Once the AI analysis is completed, the images are automatically discarded.

7. Data Deletion

Since the application does not require user accounts and does not permanently store personal data, users can delete any data by:

  • Deleting scanned images from their device
  • Clearing the app data
  • Uninstalling the application

Users may also contact us to request data removal.

8. Children's Privacy

The application is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect user information.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page.
